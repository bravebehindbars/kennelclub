Massachusetts Institute Kennel Club Updates

Our Timeline

Your experience matters to us.

  • 2003-2006

    Branding Our Vision

    We began with an idea that blossomed into a goal and our team put into action. MIKC are of the firm belief that dogs can help alleviate some of the social stresses experienced by those diagnosed with ASD. We want to be a champion for those who are marginalized or perceived as "weird" due to ASD. We see you, we love you and we are here to help.

  • June 2006

    A Dream Come True

    Our company got the funding from multiple donors and was enveloped by important charities to which their support was essential to begin in achieving our goals. Our mission is to assist in bettering the lives of those afflicted with certain social ineptitudes caused by ASD. We provide dogs with specific training to meet the child's needs in social situations, which can ensure a better life and experience for all our clients.

  • April 2012

    We Launch Our Services Countrywide

    We established our headquaters and are now fully staffed with experts. The professionals at MIKC cover all areas needed to accomplish our goals while conquering our dreams. We are experts in all things dog and work in harmony to make any transition as smooth as possible for our clients. Due to our laser focus and quality care, our services have been greatly in demand all across the country. We are fully commited to making every client's experience memorable and exciting.

  • September 2019

    Phase Two Expansion

    We now posses different locations and have expanded our clientele fivefold to our original projections. The matches are being made and both MIKC and our clients are ecstatic of the changes that we have all witnessed in children's lives. Seeing the transformation a child can make in a social setting with the assistance of a dog(whether a service dog or pet), keeps MIKC mindful of why it is that we do what we do.
